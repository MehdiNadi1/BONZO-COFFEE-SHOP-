THE
BONZO
STORY

01 / Culture

Bonzo is a contemporary coffee shop rooted in Essaouira’s Medina, created for people who want more than just a quick coffee. It’s a place where quality drinks, fresh food, and a warm, relaxed atmosphere come together to turn simple breaks into memorable moments.

02 / Community

Designed as a social, easygoing space, Bonzo blends the energy of a neighborhood spot with the feel of a modern café. Whether you are catching up with friends, taking a pause between city walks, or settling in for a slow moment, the space is made to feel welcoming, comfortable, and effortlessly cool.

03 / Craft

At Bonzo, every detail matters — from the coffee and the cups to the lighting, the vibe, and the people behind the counter. More than a café, Bonzo is a place to meet, recharge, and enjoy good energy in the heart of the city.
